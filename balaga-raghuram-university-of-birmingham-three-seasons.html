University of Birmingham - Three Seasons

The University of Birmingham is one of the most beautiful red brick universities in the UK, and I was lucky enough to experience its campus across all three seasons. In summer the campus is full of green trees and warm sunlight, in winter the iconic clock tower stands tall surrounded by a blanket of white snow, and in spring the whole place comes alive again with fresh blooms and clear blue skies. Every season gives this historic campus a completely different feel, and it is truly amazing to see how the same buildings and pathways transform throughout the year.
