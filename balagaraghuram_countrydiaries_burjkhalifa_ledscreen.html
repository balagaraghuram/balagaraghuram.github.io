balaga raghuram - country diaries - Burj Khalifa wonders

The Burj Khalifa, standing at an awe-inspiring 828 meters, holds the title of the tallest building in the world. An architectural marvel, it dominates the Dubai skyline, offering breathtaking panoramic views from its observation decks.

Visitors can journey to the 124th and 148th floors for unparalleled vistas of the city, desert, and sea. By day, the tower glistens in the sunlight; by night, it transforms into a sparkling beacon, illuminated by the world's largest LED screen, which covers much of its façade and hosts dazzling light shows that can be seen from miles away.

Surrounding the Burj Khalifa, the Dubai Fountain performs choreographed water shows set to music, making the area a vibrant hub of culture and entertainment.
