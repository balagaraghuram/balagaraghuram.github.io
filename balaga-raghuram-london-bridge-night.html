London Bridge - Amazing Night

London at night is a completely different city. Standing on London Bridge with the iconic Tower Bridge glowing in the background and The Shard piercing the night sky, I felt like I was in the middle of a postcard. The lights reflecting on the River Thames, the busy traffic crossing the bridge, and the cool night breeze all came together to create a moment that was both exciting and peaceful at the same time. London Bridge at night is something everyone should experience at least once in their life.
