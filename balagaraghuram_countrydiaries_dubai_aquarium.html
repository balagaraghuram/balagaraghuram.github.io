Dubai Aquarium Wonders

November 16, 2023 • by Balaga Raghuram

The Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, located in the heart of The Dubai Mall, is one of the largest aquariums in the world. With a massive 10-million-liter tank housing thousands of aquatic animals, including sharks, rays, and colorful reef fish, it offers a breathtaking view into the wonders of the ocean.

Visitors can walk through a 48-meter-long glass tunnel for a 270-degree view of marine life swimming overhead and around them. The experience feels like stepping into an underwater paradise, surrounded by vibrant coral reefs and exotic sea creatures.

The Underwater Zoo adds another layer of adventure, featuring fascinating exhibits of rare aquatic species.
