Early Morning in Edinburgh, Scotland

Early Morning in Edinburgh, Scotland

Waking up early in Edinburgh was one of the best decisions I made during my trip. The quiet morning streets, the soft light hitting the old stone buildings, and the mist hanging over the Gothic architecture made the whole city feel like something out of a movie. Edinburgh is a place where every corner has history, where the medieval old town meets the elegant new town, and where you can feel the spirit of Scotland in every step you take.
