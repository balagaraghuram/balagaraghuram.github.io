Canary Wharf, London

Canary Wharf, London

Standing at the edge of the dock at Canary Wharf, with the glass giants of London's financial district towering above and their reflections shimmering on the calm water, I felt the true energy of this incredible city. The blend of modern architecture with the historic docklands heritage makes this place truly special, it is where London's maritime past meets its ambitious future, and every corner has something stunning to offer.
